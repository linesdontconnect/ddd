Welcome to Gotham University

Learn, explore, and grow in the heart of Gotham City.

Mission & Vision

Mission: To educate students to make Gotham a better city.

Vision: To create a safe and smart Gotham through learning.

History

Gotham University was founded in 1856. It survived the Great Gotham Fire and grew into one of the most respected schools in the city. The Wayne family helped build labs and research centers for students.

Academic Programs

  • Criminology & Forensic Sciences
  • Engineering & Applied Physics
  • Literature & Social Sciences
  • Medicine
  • Business & Law

Campus Landmarks

  • Robinson Hall – Humanities Department
  • Wayne Research Center – Tech labs
  • Gotham Botanical Greenhouse
  • Founders Plaza – Statues of founders
